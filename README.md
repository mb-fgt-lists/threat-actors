# threat-actors
Holds lists of threat actors as lists of IP addresses or domain names.

These lists can be ingested by firewalls to block known ranges or domains that are not properly handled by vendor databases or are operating in a grey area.

If you use this lists in you firewalls, it's **your** responsibility.
These lists are updated on a irregular basis and **do not** make up for skipping subscription services of your firewall vendor.
They **do** work around quirks encountered in everyday operational realities, which may be subjective or not even apply to you at all.
